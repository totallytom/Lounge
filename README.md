# Lounge

# What it's about
Have you looked for a place to escape from the social media glaze? Have you looked for a space for yourself only?
This is Lounge. Currently a prototype digital room and a social room for you and your friends.

# Features
* You have a room.
* You can decorate your room.
* You can decorate your avatar.
* You can invite your friends over.
* You can earn lunas (digital point system).
* You can purchase items with lunas that you've earned.
* You can chat with your friends with in-app direct message.
* You can write your own journal. Private it or Publicize what you wrote.
* You can post images on your journal. Decorate your journal with as much images. Just like a book.
* You can live or private your account in StreetPass Discovery, a feature where you can find nearby users.
